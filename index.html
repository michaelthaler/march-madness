Upset Bonus

Correctly picking an upset earns you bonus points. An upset is when a higher-seeded team (the underdog) beats a lower-seeded team (the favorite). The bigger the upset, the bigger the bonus.

For every seed of difference between the winner and loser, you get an extra 10% of the round's base points. So if you correctly pick a 10 seed to beat a 6 seed in the Sweet 16, that's a difference of 4 seeds:

50 base + 50 × (10% × 4) = 50 + 20 = 70 points

A 12 seed beating a 5 seed in the Round of 64? That's a 7-seed difference:

20 base + 20 × (10% × 7) = 20 + 14 = 34 points

There's no bonus for picking a favorite to win — only underdogs earn the extra points.
